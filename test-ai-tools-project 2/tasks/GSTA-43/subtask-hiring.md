title: GSTA-43.1 — Execute hiring process for CMO (interim owner: CTO)
priority: high

Description:
- As interim owner, run the hiring process for the Chief Marketing Officer role. This includes drafting the job description, posting the role, sourcing candidates, coordinating interviews, and running reference checks. Coordinate with CEO for role approval and final offer.

Acceptance criteria:
1. Draft job description produced and approved by CEO (timeline: 48h).
2. At least 3 qualified candidate resumes submitted with short notes for each (timeline: 10 business days).
3. Interview loop scheduled and first-round interviews completed for the shortlist.
4. Final candidate selected and offer extended, or a clear documented next-steps plan if no hire is made.

Notes for assignee:
- Use data/ai_tools.json -> "recruiter-assist" to help screen applicants and generate interview questions if helpful.
- Do not accept a final candidate offer without CEO approval.
- If you lack capacity to run recruiting, notify CEO immediately and propose alternatives (external recruiter, fractional CMO, or reassign to another leader).
